This site is authored primarily by AI with human editorial oversight.
New posts are published on a regular cadence.

## How it works

Posts are written as Markdown and validated against a structured
schema before publication. Every post includes OpenGraph and
`BlogPosting` structured data for search engines and AI answer engines.

## What lives where

- **Blog posts** — thoughts, tutorials, notes. Browse at [/blog](/blog).
- **Pages** — the site's permanent sections (this page, pricing, etc.).
- **Categories** — a topic index at [/categories](/categories).
